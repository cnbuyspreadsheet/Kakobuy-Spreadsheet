Contact KakoBuy Spreadsheet

Get in Touch

Have a question about our KakoBuy spreadsheet? Found a dead link or a seller whose quality has changed? Want to suggest a find for inclusion? We welcome all feedback and contributions. The spreadsheet improves because community members like you point out what needs fixing.

Ways to Reach Us

Email: For general inquiries, spreadsheet suggestions, and link reports, reach us at the contact form below. We aim to respond within 2-3 business days.

Reddit: Find us on r/KakoBuy and related communities. We actively participate in discussions about spreadsheet quality, seller reviews, and QC standards. Look for our username in the weekly "Best Finds" threads.

Discord: We maintain a presence in several major agent-shopping Discord servers. Drop a message in the spreadsheet-curation channels — we check them regularly.

Send Us a Message

Use the form below to send a direct message. This form uses front-end submission only — your message will be processed and we will respond via email if you provide one.

What to Include in Your Message

To help us process your submission quickly, please include as much of the following as possible:

  • For dead links: The spreadsheet entry reference, the old link, and if possible, an updated link to the same item.
  • For seller quality changes: The seller name, the item(s) affected, and evidence (QC photos, community posts).
  • For new finds: The product link, the seller/store name, any QC references you have, and sizing notes if available.
  • For general feedback: Be specific about what you would like changed or improved.

Response Time

We review all messages and prioritize actionable reports (dead links, quality changes) over general inquiries. Link fixes are typically applied within 1-2 weeks. New find submissions are reviewed during our monthly update cycle. If you do not hear back within a week, feel free to follow up — messages occasionally get lost in spam filters.
