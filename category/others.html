KakoBuy Others: The Category of Unexpected Gems

Not everything fits neatly into shoes, hoodies, or accessories. The KakoBuy others section of our spreadsheet is the catch-all for finds that defy categorization — home goods, tech gadgets, desk accessories, toys, pet supplies, automotive accessories, and novelty items. This is often where the most interesting and unique finds live, because they fly under the radar of mainstream spreadsheet curators.

What Lives in "Others"

In 2026, the others category on KakoBuy has grown significantly. Common finds include desk mats and mousepads, LED lighting, phone stands, car interior accessories, plush toys, kitchen gadgets, camping gear, and pet clothing. The quality spread is enormous — some items are genuinely excellent at a fraction of Western retail prices; others are disposable junk. Our spreadsheet applies the same QC rigor to this category: verified sellers, community-reviewed listings, and honest quality notes.

Watch OutElectronics in the "others" category (chargers, cables, adapters) carry safety risks. Only buy electronics from sellers with extensive positive reviews. A ¥15 charger that damages your ¥1000 phone is not a bargain.

Shipping Notes for Miscellaneous Items

Miscellaneous items vary enormously in weight and volume. A desk mat is light but bulky (high volumetric weight). A set of kitchen tools is dense (high actual weight). Use the KakoBuy shipping calculator to estimate costs before ordering — the surprise shipping bill on an oddly-shaped item can exceed the item price.
