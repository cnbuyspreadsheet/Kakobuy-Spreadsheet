How to Order from KakoBuy — Complete Step-by-Step Walkthrough (2026)

Published: April 20, 2026Updated: May 26, 2026

Before You Start: Prepare Your Spreadsheet

The single best thing you can do before ordering from KakoBuy is build a spreadsheet. Create columns for category, size target, weight estimate, QC status, and notes. Having this open while you browse keeps you disciplined.

Step by Step: Browsing to Delivery

Research categories using our guides → add promising items to your spreadsheet → place orders → review warehouse QC photos → approve or reject each item → consolidate approved items → select shipping line → pay for international shipping → track your package → receive and inspect.

  1. Build Your Spreadsheet

    Open any spreadsheet tool. Add columns: Category, Item, Size Target, Weight Est., QC Status, Notes. This is your control center.

  2. Research Categories

    Read our shoes, hoodies, and other category guides to understand what to look for.

  3. Browse and Select

    Open the full directory. Add promising items to your spreadsheet. Do not order yet — just research.

  4. Place Orders

    When your spreadsheet is solid, place your orders. Items ship to the KakoBuy warehouse (1-3 days domestic).

  5. Review QC Photos

    Warehouse photos arrive. Compare against listing images and your spreadsheet criteria. Mark Passed or RL.

  6. Consolidate and Ship

    Approve consolidation. Select shipping line. Apply coupons if available. Pay for international shipping.

Frequently Asked Questions

Start with 3-5 items across 2-3 categories. Keep it manageable. A good first order: one pair of shoes, one hoodie, one accessory.

Warehouse processing: 5-7 days. International shipping: 5-25 days depending on line. Total: approximately 2-5 weeks from order to delivery.
