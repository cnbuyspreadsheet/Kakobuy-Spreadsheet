KakoBuy Beginner Guide — Everything You Need for Your First Haul (2026)

Published: April 20, 2026Updated: May 26, 2026

Welcome to KakoBuy — Start Here

If you are brand new to KakoBuy, this is your starting point. The platform works, the community is active, and the spreadsheet method is proven. But jumping in without preparation is the fastest path to disappointment. This guide covers everything a first-time user needs — in order.

Your First Haul Checklist

1) Build a spreadsheet with core columns. 2) Read the shoes guide and one other category guide. 3) Pick 3-5 items across 2-3 categories. 4) Add weight estimates to your spreadsheet. 5) Browse, compare, and only order when your spreadsheet looks solid. 6) Review QC carefully. 7) Use the shipping calculator before paying.

What to Expect Timeline-Wise

Domestic shipping to warehouse: 1-3 days. QC processing: 2-4 days. Consolidation: 1-2 days. International shipping: 5-25 days (depending on line). Customs: 1-5 days. Total: 2-5 weeks. Set realistic expectations and you will not be frustrated by normal delays.
