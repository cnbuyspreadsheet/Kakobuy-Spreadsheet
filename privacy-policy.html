Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

1. Introduction

KakoBuy Spreadsheet ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website at https://cnbuyspreadsheet.github.io/Kakobuy-Spreadsheet. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website. By using our site, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information. Our website is a static site with no user accounts, no databases, and no server-side processing. The information we may collect includes:

  • Contact form submissions: When you use our contact form, you voluntarily provide your name, email address, and message content. This information is processed through a front-end mechanism and is not stored in a database.
  • Analytics: We may use privacy-focused, cookieless analytics to understand site traffic. This does not involve personal data collection or tracking across sites.
  • Server logs: Our hosting provider may automatically log standard technical information (IP address, browser type, pages visited, timestamp). These logs are used for operational purposes and are not linked to individual identities.

3. Cookies

Our website uses a single functional cookie to remember your theme preference (light/dark mode). This cookie contains only a preference value ("light" or "dark") and does not track you across sites or collect personal information. You can clear this cookie at any time through your browser settings. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, or third-party cookies.

4. How We Use Information

Information collected through the contact form is used solely to respond to your inquiry. We do not use contact form submissions for marketing, do not add submitters to mailing lists without explicit consent, and do not share submissions with third parties. Server logs are used for security monitoring, error diagnosis, and traffic analysis.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties. The only exception is where required by law (e.g., responding to a valid court order) or to protect the security and integrity of our website.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website contains links to external sites including KakoBuy, Weidian, Taobao, AstroReps, and Reddit. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. When you click an external link, you are subject to that site's privacy policy, not ours. We recommend reviewing the privacy policy of any site you visit.

7. Data Security

As a static website, we have no user database to breach. Contact form submissions are handled through client-side processing. Server logs are protected by our hosting provider's security measures. While no online transmission is 100% secure, the minimal data we handle reduces the attack surface significantly.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including GDPR in the EU/EEA and CCPA in California), you may have rights regarding your personal data, including the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing. Since we collect minimal data and do not maintain user accounts, exercising these rights is straightforward — contact us and we will address your request.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the most recent changes took effect. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your data rights, please contact us through the contact page or email us at the contact form.
